About Us

Satsang Vihar Gorakhpur is a religious organization which is promoting Dharm, which upholds the existence and growth, to every individual irrespective of age, gender, race, religion, national origin and harmonious relations among the persons of different faiths to lead a good life in diverse society as envisaged by SREE SREE THAKUR ANUKULCHANDRA in consultation with PARAM PUJYAPAD SREE SREE ACHARYADEV of Satsang, Deoghar, INDIA.
